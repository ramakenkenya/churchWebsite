About Us

PAG Mbale City Church was founded in 1961 when a couple (Mr/Ms. Doud) from Canada staged an evangelistic open-air campaign that saw the extraordinary move of the Holy Spirit. This took place on plot 43a Kumi Road Mbale Municipality (which is now Mbale City) The blind saw, the lame walked and many other miracles accompanied the gospel.

Later with the help from Canada and a few Swiss churches, the first permanent church building was erected to hold a population of 400 people. The same structure served as the national headquarters for PAG Uganda till 2001 when the PAG headquarters were shifted to Kampala, Bugolobi.

On 27th 2006. PAG Mbale City Church relocated her services to plot 5-7 Buginyanya close (opposite Mbale Parents Secondary School) Buwalasi cell, Half London, in the Northern division, Mbale City The relocation of the church has generated numerous opportunities to reach schools, hostels, and families with the Gospel.
